Get help with billing, platform bugs, and account issues through Discord. 

Community support is available on Discord.[**​**](https://docs.lovable.dev/introduction/support-policy#scope)

### **Scope**

Nilo's support covers the following areas:

- Billing issues
- Platform bugs
- Account management
- Workarounds or solutions to known issues
- General guidance on using Nilo
- General questions about Nilo

Nilo's support does not cover:

- Project-specific debugging
- Guidance with implementation of third-party integrations
